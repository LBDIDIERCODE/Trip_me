Hotel Eden

Located in the heart of Rome, near the Spanish Steps, Hotel Eden is a luxurious five-star hotel offering breathtaking views of the city. It features elegant rooms and suites, a rooftop terrace with panoramic views, a Michelin-starred restaurant, and a spa.

Ritz Paris

Located in the heart of Paris, the Ritz Paris is a historic and iconic hotel that has been synonymous with luxury for over a century. The hotel offers elegantly designed rooms and suites, Michelin-starred dining options, a world-class spa, and impeccable service. It has welcomed numerous celebrities, politicians, and royalty over the years and is known for its opulence and attention to detail.

Hotel Bristol

a Luxury Collection Hotel, Warsaw: Located in the heart of Warsaw, Hotel Bristol is a historic and prestigious five-star hotel.
